Our Philosophy

Conscious Luxury

Time-Honored

We respect the maceration process. Our perfumes rest for 6 weeks before bottling to ensure depth and stability.

Ethical Botany

We prohibit endangered species. All musks are synthetic or bio-identical, cruelty-free, and clean.

Artistic Freedom

No focus groups. No marketing briefs. Just pure, unadulterated artistic expression from the perfumer.
