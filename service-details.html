Bespoke Blending

The ultimate personal luxury: a fragrance designed exclusively for you, by you, with our guidance.

Book Consultation

The Experience

Our Bespoke Blending session is an immersive journey into the world of scent. Over the course of two hours, you will sit with a Master Perfumer to explore your olfactory preferences, memories, and desires.

You will have access to our full perfume organ containing over 500 raw materials, from the finest floral absolutes to rare woods and resins. We will guide you through the structure of perfume—top, heart, and base notes—to construct a balanced and harmonious scent that is uniquely yours.

What's Included

  • 2-hour private consultation
  • Scent profile analysis
  • 50ml Eau de Parfum of your creation
  • Formula preservation for future refills
  • Champagne and refreshments
